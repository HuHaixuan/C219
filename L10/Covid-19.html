The number of Covid-19 cases in Singapore has grown to 257,510, with 2,079 new cases reported on 14 Dec.

Of these, 2,030 were reported in the community and 40 in the workers' domitories. The remaining nine cases were imported.

A further 2,275 patients were discharged, including 366 seniors aged above 60. A total of 238,909 cases have recovered to date.

Vaccination Progress



For every 100 people in Singapore, 88 have recived their first dose and 87 are fully vaccinacted as at 14 Dec.

In addition, the Health Ministry reported that as at 14 Dec, 96 percent of the eligible population (aged 12 years and above) have completed their full regime, or recived two doses of Covid-19 vaccines.

With new Covid-19 variants such as Omicorn surfacing, full vaccination will have to include booster shots as well.
